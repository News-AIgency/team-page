WRAITE

Generate news articles from verified sources with AI

About the project

Nowadays, a lot of information is processed. This is also the case in the news industry, where journalists do not always manage to report information as quickly as it is published on the Internet. Journalists often do not manage to process this data and report it appropriately within the required timeframe. In this project, we work on creating an application that will use artificial intelligence to automatically generate news from verified sources. Thus, the application will act as a journalist's assistant to help generate news articles that could be generated from the trusted sources of information. The journalist can then modify the generated results in order to get closer to his idea of the final article. The use of generative artificiall intelligence would make it easier to write the reports and they could be published more quickly. By using relevant sources and mentioning them in the final report, the application will also combat the spread of misinformation.
